About

Hi, my name is Billy, I'm a 22 year old games programmer from England. I would describe myself as someone who is unable to rest. I'm always seeking bigger and better things which helps me to rapidly improve my skills. This drive brought me from my first simple text-based adventure game in Python to a fully-released commercial title on Steam, hopefully with many more to come. One of my goals now is to work with other developers to learn new things create bigger games. I am also a Guitar player (you might've noticed), I love playing songs by Tommy Emmanuel and Molly Tuttle.
